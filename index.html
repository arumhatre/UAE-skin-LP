03What are the typical signs of vitiligo?

Typical signs of vitiligo include:
  • White patches on the skin, which can appear anywhere on the body
  • Premature whitening or greying of the hair on your scalp, eyelashes, eyebrows, or beard
  • Loss of colour in the tissues inside your mouth and nose
  • Changes in the colour of the retina (inner layer of the eyeball)
